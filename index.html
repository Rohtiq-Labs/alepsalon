Who We Are

"We don't just cut hair. We shape how you present yourself to the world."

Alep Salon is a modern barber studio rooted in Biel, Switzerland. We blend the clean elegance of French barbering with Swiss attention to detail — the result is precise, consistent, and unmistakably sharp.

Every client who sits in our chair leaves looking polished and feeling confident. Whether it's your weekly maintenance or a fresh transformation, we bring the same care and craft to every cut.
